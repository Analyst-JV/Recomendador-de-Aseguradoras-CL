    "# Metodología de Score(definiciones breves + fórmulas)\n",
    "\n",
    "\n",
    "## 1) Score  — *(0–100)*\n",
    "\n",
    "* **Qué es:** media **geométrica ponderada** de Cobertura (10%), Tiempo (40%) e IEC (40%), trabajando todo en 0–1.\n",
    "* **Por qué importa:** exige **desempeño balanceado**; un factor muy bajo reduce fuertemente el score.\n",
    "\n",
    "```math\n",
    "\\text{Score} \\;=\\; 100 \\cdot \\exp\\!\\left(\\frac{0.10\\,\\ln c + 0.40\\,\\ln t + 0.40\\,\\ln i}{0.10 + 0.40 + 0.40}\\right),\n",
    "\\quad c\\in[0,1],\\; t=\\text{Tiempo}/100,\\; i=\\text{IEC}/100.\n",
    "```\n",
    "\n",
    "---\n",
    "\n",
    "## 2) IEC — *(0–100)*\n",
    "\n",
    "* **Qué es:** índice compuesto **compensatorio** (calidad y resolución).\n",
    "* **Por qué importa:** sintetiza señales clave en una sola métrica.\n",
    "\n",
    "```math\n",
    "\\text{IEC} \\;=\\; 0.35\\,FR \\;+\\; 0.45\\,CRS \\;+\\; 0.20\\,RC\n",
    "```\n",
    "\n",
    "---\n",
    "\n",
    "## 3) CRS Score (Bruto) — *(0–100)*\n",
    "\n",
    "* **Qué es:** calidad de **resolución** según el mix de resultados.\n",
    "* **Por qué importa:** premia **acoger**/**resolver**; penaliza **no responder**.\n",
    "\n",
    "```math\n",
    "\\text{CRS} \\;=\\; 100 \\cdot\n",
    "\\frac{1.00\\,s_a \\;+\\; 0.35\\,s_{np} \\;+\\; 0.20\\,s_{na} \\;+\\; 0\\cdot s_{nr}}\n",
    "{s_a \\;+\\; s_{np} \\;+\\; s_{na} \\;+\\; s_{nr}}\n",
    "\\qquad\n",
    "\\begin{aligned}\n",
    "&s_a:\\ \\text{acoge}\\\\\n",
    "&s_{np}:\\ \\text{no procede}\\\\\n",
    "&s_{na}:\\ \\text{no acoge}\\\\\n",
    "&s_{nr}:\\ \\text{no responde}\n",
    "\\end{aligned}\n",
    "```\n",
    "\n",
    "---\n",
    "\n",
    "## 4) FR Score (Bruto) — *(0–100)*\n",
    "\n",
    "* **Qué es:** desempeño en **tasa de reclamos** frente a benchmarks nacionales.\n",
    "* **Por qué importa:** proxy de **calidad/posventa** (menos reclamos ⇒ mejor).\n",
    "  *(Luego acotar a ([0,100])).*\n",
    "\n",
    "```math\n",
    "\\text{FR}(r) \\;=\\;\n",
    "\\begin{cases}\n",
    "50 \\;+\\; 50\\,\\dfrac{a-r}{a-m}, & r \\le a,\\\\[8pt]\n",
    "50 \\;-\\; 50\\,\\dfrac{r-a}{M-a}, & r > a,\n",
    "\\end{cases}\n",
    "\\quad\n",
    "\\begin{aligned}\n",
    "&r:& \\text{tasa compañía}\\\\\n",
    "&m,a,M:& \\text{mín, prom, máx nacionales}\n",
    "\\end{aligned}\n",
    "```\n",
    "\n",
    "---\n",
    "\n",
    "## 5) Tiempo Score (simple • TREATAS) — *(0–100)*\n",
    "\n",
    "* **Qué es:** desempeño de **tiempos promedio** por severidad vs. benchmarks; pondera más **Grave**.\n",
    "* **Por qué importa:** la **celeridad** impacta directamente la satisfacción.\n",
    "  *(Cada (S_s) se acota a ([0,100])).*\n",
    "\n",
    "```math\n",
    "S_s(r_s) \\;=\\;\n",
    "\\begin{cases}\n",
    "50 \\;+\\; 50\\,\\dfrac{a_s-r_s}{a_s-m_s}, & r_s \\le a_s,\\\\[8pt]\n",
    "50 \\;-\\; 50\\,\\dfrac{r_s-a_s}{M_s-a_s}, & r_s > a_s,\n",
    "\\end{cases}\n",
    "\\quad\n",
    "s\\in\\{\\text{Leve},\\text{Mediana},\\text{Grave}\\}\n",
    "```\n",
    "\n",
    "```math\n",
    "\\text{TiempoScore} \\;=\\; 0.20\\,S_{\\text{Leve}} \\;+\\; 0.30\\,S_{\\text{Mediana}} \\;+\\; 0.50\\,S_{\\text{Grave}}\n",
    "```\n",
    "\n",
    "---\n",
    "\n",
    "## 6) Cobertura Territorial — *(0–1)*\n",
    "\n",
    "* **Qué es:** densidad de talleres por 10k clientes, normalizada en **log** entre mínimo y máximo regional; centra el **promedio** regional en 0.5 (si es válido).\n",
    "* **Por qué importa:** refleja **acceso** y disponibilidad territorial.\n",
    "  *(Luego acotar el resultado a ([0,1])).*\n",
    "\n",
    "```math\n",
    "y=\\frac{\\ln v - \\ln lo}{\\ln hi - \\ln lo}, \n",
    "\\qquad\n",
    "y_{\\text{avg}}=\\frac{\\ln \\overline v - \\ln lo}{\\ln hi - \\ln lo}\n",
    "```\n",
    "\n",
    "```math\n",
    "\\text{Cobertura} \\;=\\;\n",
    "\\begin{cases}\n",
    "y, & y_{\\text{avg}}\\notin(0,1),\\\\[6pt]\n",
    "0.5\\,\\dfrac{y}{y_{\\text{avg}}}, & y \\le y_{\\text{avg}},\\\\[10pt]\n",
    "0.5 \\;+\\; 0.5\\,\\dfrac{y - y_{\\text{avg}}}{1 - y_{\\text{avg}}}, & y > y_{\\text{avg}}.\n",
    "\\end{cases}\n",
    "```\n",
    "\n",
    "---\n",
    "\n",
    "## 7) RC — *(0–100)*\n",
    "\n",
    "* **Qué es:** cumplimiento de **respuesta** (inverso del “no responde”).\n",
    "* **Por qué importa:** mide **responsividad** operativa.\n",
    "\n",
    "```math\n",
    "\\text{RC} \\;=\\; 100\\,\\big(1 - s_{nr}\\big), \\qquad s_{nr}\\in[0,1]\n",
    "```\n",
    "\n",
    "---\n",
    "\n",
    "## 8) Share Proveedor no responde — *(0–1)*\n",
    "\n",
    "* **Qué es:** proporción de casos en que el proveedor **no responde** (por compañía).\n",
    "* **Por qué importa:** indicador directo de **ausencia de respuesta**.\n",
    "\n",
    "```math\n",
    "s_{nr} \\;=\\;\n",
    "\\begin{cases}\n",
    "p/100, & \\text{si el dato } p \\text{ viene en \\%},\\\\[6pt]\n",
    "p, & \\text{si } p \\in [0,1],\\\\[6pt]\n",
    "0, & \\text{si no hay datos}.\n",
    "\\end{cases}\n",
    "```\n",
    "\n",
